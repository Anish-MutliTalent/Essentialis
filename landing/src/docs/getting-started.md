# Getting Started with Essentialis

Welcome to Essentialis! This guide will help you set up and use our secure document platform in just a few easy steps. Whether you’re new to digital document management or moving over from another service, you’ll find the process simple.

---

## What is Essentialis?
Essentialis is a safe place to store, organize, and share your important documents. Everything you upload is encrypted and only you have access (not even we can see your files!).

---

## Step 1: Create Your Account
- Go to the sign-up page and enter your email address or use Google login.
- Complete email verification (we’ll send you a one-time code).
- For extra security, turn on two-factor authentication. (Optional but recommended!)

---

## Step 2: Log In & Connect Your Wallet
- Log in using your email, Google, or connect a crypto wallet (like MetaMask).
- If you haven’t set up a wallet, follow our guided flow – it’s beginner-friendly!

---

## Step 3: Upload Your First Document
- Press "Upload" on your dashboard.
- Choose files from your computer or drag them in.
- Add tags or categories to keep things organized.

---

## Step 4: Explore & Organize
- Use folders, tags, or the search bar to quickly find your docs.
- Try out the preview mode before downloading.

---

## Step 5: Share (When You’re Ready)
- Generate secure links to share documents with others.
- You can password-protect, set expiry times, and revoke access anytime.

---

## Tips for New Users
- All data is encrypted end-to-end; privacy is our top priority.
- Lost access? Our recovery options are simple but secure—follow on-screen steps.
- Help is one click away via our support page!
