# Getting Started with Essentials

Learn how to set up your account and start storing your confidential documents securely.

---

### Create Your Account
Sign up for Essentialis Cloud with your email address. We use industry-standard encryption to protect your login credentials.

---

### Verify Your Identity
Complete the verification process to ensure the security of your account. This includes email verification and optional two-factor authentication.

---

### Upload Your First Document
Start by uploading a test document to familiarize yourself with our secure upload process and encryption features.
