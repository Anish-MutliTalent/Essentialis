# Accessing Your Documents

Learn how to view, download, and manage your stored documents.

---

### View Online
Preview most document types directly in your browser without downloading, maintaining security through encrypted viewing.

---

### Download Securely
Download documents to your device with automatic decryption. Files are decrypted locally on your device.

---

### Search and Filter
Use our powerful search functionality to quickly find documents by name, tags, or content.
