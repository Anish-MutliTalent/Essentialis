# Managing Your Account

Here’s how you can make the most of your Essentialis profile and keep your information safe.

---

## Profile Settings
- Edit your name or email anytime via the Profile page.
- Update your password in the Security section.
- Control what email notifications you get (reminders, shares, etc.).

---

## Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)
- Turn 2FA on to make your account much safer.
- Choose between SMS or an authenticator app (like Google Authenticator or Authy).
- We walk you through each setup step with QR codes and simple instructions.
- You can disable 2FA later if needed, but we recommend leaving it enabled!

---

## Storage and Plan Management
- See how much cloud storage you’ve used and what’s left.
- Upgrade in 1 click if you need more room.
- Download a usage report anytime from your dashboard.

---

## Safe Account Recovery
- Forgotten your password or lost access? Our secure flow will verify your identity and help you recover safely.
- Still stuck? Contact support—we’ll help, but never breach your privacy.
